**Conclusion:** `mcp_tools=0` here = Claude *declined* the graph on an easy task, not a dead server. Therefore if A2 (cross-repo) also shows cgc with `mcp_tools=0`, that is a genuine finding (graph unused / not reached for), not a silent breakage. The cross-repo result can be trusted.
